About this event

Most buyers chase the hottest launch. Smart buyers look one step earlier — at the land.

When a developer acquires land at a lower cost, that savings doesn't disappear. It becomes headroom: room to price competitively, room for future appreciation, room for you to enter before the market catches on.

This session breaks down exactly how to spot those projects — and why the window to act is always shorter than you think.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is this seminar really free?
Yes — completely free. There is no entry fee, no hidden charges, and no obligation to purchase anything. This is a consumer education seminar by PropNex, designed to help you make better property decisions.
Will there be a hard sell at the event?
No. PropNex CES seminars are structured as educational sessions. Our agents may be present, but the session is focused on market knowledge and frameworks — not product pitching. You can leave at any time, no questions asked.
What if I can't make it on the day?
Let us know in advance and we'll cancel your reservation so your seat can go to someone else. PropNex regularly hosts similar seminars — we'll notify you of upcoming events via SMS or WhatsApp.
Can I bring a friend or family member?
Absolutely — and we encourage it. Each person must register separately to secure their own seat. Use the WhatsApp share button to send them the registration link directly.
Is parking available?
Yes, free parking is available on-site at the Tengah Garden Residences showflat. We recommend arriving by 7:00 PM as spaces fill up quickly.
How long is the seminar?
The session runs from 7:30 PM to 9:00 PM — approximately 90 minutes including a Q&A segment at the end. Light refreshments will be provided.
